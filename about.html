Who is PABH Candle?


PABH is a candle company started by five men in the heart of Idaho. Coming from the four corners of the USA. A casual friendship sparked and they came together to serve their customers creating the best smelling candles known to mankind. Our goals are to deliver a superior hand crafted product unlike any before. We aim to become the number one candle provider in the world.


Meet the Owners

P

JT Pyle is a college student in Rexburg Idaho from Arizona, Omaha Nebraska, and Rareden Ohio. As he grew up it was well known in his home that his mother would light a candle so when he got home he was introduced to the smells of the seasons. On every summer he would go to his grandparent's house and work on the family homestead. Smells has been a key link to JT's life and when the company came in as an idea he was on board so he could share the smells of his childhood to the world.

A

Josiah Aponte is a Lawyer graduated from Crighton University. Growing up in Flordia he desired greatness and for an oppourtunity to lead driven people. Working as a procecuting attourney he one day after church was talking about business ideas when the idea for candles came up. He said simply when asked why he wanted to start the business, "I recognized great talents in my friends and that we could together deliver an amazing product that would benefit peoples lives."

B

Kevin Baxter orginally wanted to start a candle company with his brothers but being under 18 they had no way to contribute to the business. When the Candle company's oppourtunity presented itself he had to be their. Kevin is a hard dedicated intense young man with passion and is a great addition to our team.

H

John Hayword is a college student in Rexburg Idaho going for a degree in marketing. He has been involved in creating businesses on campus and has became known for creating successful candle companies on campus. The direction he chose to take was to become an entrepenuer and give the world a stunning product with a team of equally driven individuals.
